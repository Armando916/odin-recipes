Pancake

Description

Pancakes are a popular breakfast dish made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder, cooked on a hot griddle or pan. They are light, fluffy, and golden-brown on the outside, often served with toppings like syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream. Whether enjoyed plain or filled with chocolate chips or berries, pancakes are a comforting and versatile treat loved by many for their soft texture and sweet flavor.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • milk
  • baking powder

Steps

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, whisk together flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder until smooth.
  2. Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  3. Flip and Cook: Carefully flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden brown. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  4. Serve and Enjoy: Stack the pancakes on a plate and top with your favorite toppings. Drizzle with syrup, add a dollop of butter, or sprinkle with fruit. Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
